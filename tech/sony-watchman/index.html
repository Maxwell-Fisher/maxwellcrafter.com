Sony Watchman

Maxwell Fisher 2020-03-17



This is the Sony FD-10A, more commonly called the Sony Pocket Watchman, which is a small handheld analog television from the Sony Watchman series. The FD-10A was made for 2 years in 1986 and 1987, with this exact one being manufactured in August of 1986. The Watchman series was introduced in 1982, and ended in 2000
